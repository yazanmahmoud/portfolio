About Me

I am a web designer and AI-focused technologist with over 11 years of experience delivering user-centered digital solutions across diverse industries. My professional background combines long-term hands-on experience in web design and development with recent formal training and applied work in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science. This blend allows me to approach problems from both a design and analytical perspective, ensuring solutions are not only functional but also efficient, scalable, and user-focused.
