Welcome to My Portfolio

I design and build digital experiences where clean interfaces meet intelligent systems. With 11+ years in web design and development, I’ve spent my career turning ideas into functional, user-focused products—and recently expanded that journey into AI, Machine Learning, and Data Science.

From crafting responsive websites with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React to analyzing AI-generated outputs using Python, NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn, and PyTorch, I enjoy working at the intersection of design, data, and problem-solving. I’m especially drawn to understanding why things work (or don’t) and improving them through iteration and insight.

Currently, I work as an AI Data Analyst, where I evaluate data quality, uncover patterns in AI behavior, and help shape more reliable and human-centered systems. I’ve collaborated in remote, Agile teams and thrive in environments that value clarity, experimentation, and thoughtful execution.

I’m always curious, always building, and always looking for meaningful challenges that push products—and people—forward.
